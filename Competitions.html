Competitions
We have a wide variety of competitions that students can choose from. Whether you are into architecture or programming, or even fashion design, we have the perfect competition for you.
Architectural Design
Teams create both a digital and a physical model of a building in this exciting architectural competition.
Video Games Design
Participants test the limits of their imagination - and their coding skills - in this contest.
Children Story
By writing and illustrating a children's story, participants simultaneously express their creativity and raise awareness of global issues.
Dragster Design
Participants design and build an innovative dragster powered by CO2 to solve global challenges.
Essays on Technology
Participants make and articulate connections between different technological topics, using the real-world skill of communication.
Extemporaneous Speech
By giving improvised speeches about tech topics, students improve their communication skills while keeping current on tech news.
Coding
Writing code to solve a software challenge in an innovative way is great fun for the students in this competition.
Transportation Modeling
Participants design a vehicle and build a scale model for this thrilling competition.
Webmaster
In this competition, teams create a website to advertise their school and its TSA chapter.
