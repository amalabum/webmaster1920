Why Electric?





Unlike conventional gas cars, electric vehicles emit far less greenhouse gases, saving the planet from air pollution.

In fact, the US government is aware of that, and will give buyers of electric vehicles up to $7,500 in tax credits.

Public charging stations for electric vehicles are becoming increasingly common as more EVs cruise the streets. Moreover, you could even go solar and produce your own electricity for your car, further cutting your costs.
