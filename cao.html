Pi Club
Pi Club provides members with lessons on problem-solving techniques as well as math subjects not usually covered in school. We get together to solve hard problems, crunching numbers and trying different strategies before giving up. We encourage each other to try new things, to persist in the face of difficult problems. Together, we all get better at math each day, a little at a time. We even provide homework help!
Pi Club meets every Wednesday from 2:30 to 3:30 in room H207.
Science Club
Scientists come speak at this club, sharing their passion for research with us students. We learn about subjects ranging from molecular biology to astrophysics, and sometimes even do experiments on these subjects. Occasionally, we will also have student speakers share their knowledge for some particular scientific subject, or talk about their out-of-school scientific endeavors.
The science club meets every other Monday from 2:30 to 3:30 in room A211.
STEM Club
A combined computer science and engineering club, the STEM club lets students come together to bond over STEM subjects. This club hosts TSA at our school, helping students make teams for competitions and taking care of all logistical necessities.
STEM Club meets every Friday from 2:30 to 3:30 in room H207.
Robotics Club
In robotics clubs, students can get together to build robots to perform challenging tasks. The robotics team pulls through all the frustrations and obstacles, creating a remarkable product worthy of pride.
This club meets every Tuesday and Thursday from 2:30 to 3:15 in room H207.
